Mega Sceptile by みずのごけ
Mega Sceptile is Very Cool!

The Forest Pokémon Sceptile is one of the Hoenn starter Grass-type Pokémon from Gen 3. By gaining Dragon-typing, it joins its compatriots Mega Sceptile and Mega Blaziken in being a dual-type Pokémon. This fan-favourite is clearly inspired by geckos.
